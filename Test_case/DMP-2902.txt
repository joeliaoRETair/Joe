__DMP-2902 Business report select multiple options error
Summary:Test choose one Attribute in Business Report

Step action:
1.進入Business report頁面
2.選好Date後,勾選1個Attribute
3.檢查有沒有跳出Error Message,以及檢查Business Report-Label Overlapped有無出現圖表

預期結果:沒有跳出Error Message,圖表成功出現
實際結果:與預期結果相同
