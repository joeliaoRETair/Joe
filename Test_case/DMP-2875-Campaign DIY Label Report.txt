DMP-2875 [Campaign DIY Label Report] the tag type is not matched

Summary:Check tagtype in Campaign DIY Label Report
step action:
1.進入campaign DIY label report頁面
2.打開Network Monitor
3.選取date點選Apply
4.查看帶有api/report/extKey網址的tagType是否=2

預期結果:tagType=2
實際結果:與預期結果相同
